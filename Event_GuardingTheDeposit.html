Guarding The Deposit

“Guarding the Deposit” is our current Sunday morning sermon series through the books of 1+2 Timothy. This verse-by-verse, expository teaching will help you better understand true doctrine as we seek to guard ourselves against false teaching and will help you better understand your role and responsibilities within the local church.

“By the Holy Spirit who dwells within us, guard the good deposit entrusted to you.” – 2 Tim. 1:14
