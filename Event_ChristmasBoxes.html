Christmas Boxes

Pick up a box or two from our worship center to fill with toys, food, and clothing to help a child in our area have a great Christmas! These boxes will be going to Mason-Corinth Elementary School in Grant County for children in preschool through 5th grades (ages 4-11). You will find in the box a list of items to buy/not to buy. This is a great way to help those in need! Boxes are due back December 10th.
