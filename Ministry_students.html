Student Ministry

"Let no one despise you for your youth"

Let no one despise you for your youth, but set the believers an example in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith, in purity. – 1 Tim. 4:12 (ESV)

The student ministries of Living hope Baptist church exists to glorify God through partnering with families to raise the next generation for Christ. We equip parents to disciple their children and teach our youth everything from fundamentals of Christianity to apologetics. We provide an atmosphere where our teens can be honest, heard, accepted, and equipped to proclaim the Gospel.

We plan activities for our teens to develop meaningful friendships and accountability, and we encourage them to serve within the body of Christ, using the gifts that God has given them. Our students meet regularly on Sunday mornings at 9:30 and Sunday evenings at 5:15. Check out the church calendar to see upcoming youth events.
