Senior Pastor

Brent Walker

Pictured with Sara, Silas, + Abigail

Brent is the husband to Sara and father to Silas and Abigail. He received his Master of Divinity from The Southern Baptist Theological Seminary. He has been Living Hope Baptist Church's Pastor since September 2015. He desires to magnify God’s glory in Christ in people’s minds, hearts, and wills through faithful preaching, discipleship, and shepherding.

Favorite Authors/Pastors: John MacArthur, Albert Mohler, John Piper, David Platt, R.C. Sproul, Alistair Begg, Tommy Nelson, D.A. Carson

Hobbies: Disc Golf, Georgia Bulldog sports, games and competition of all sorts

brent@livinghopebaptist.org

Music Director

Bob Turner

Pictured with Carol, Molly, + Abby

Bob was born and raised in Northern Kentucky. He accepted Christ as his Savior at the age of 8 years old. He has been involved with music for most of his life – from band to choir. Bob has been the music director at Living Hope Baptist Church for more than 25 years. He enjoys leading the congregation in worship along with the choir and praise band. Bob is married to Carol. They have two daughters, Molly and Abby, who serve the Lord. He has worked for a Fortune 500 company in the IT field for over 30 years. Bob graduated from Northern Kentucky University with a bachelor’s degree in Mathematics.

bob@livinghopebaptist.org

Bill Lanham

Administrative Assistant

Bill co-leads our youth ministries. He desires to help parents disciple their children to see them develop into Christian maturity. Bill was a teacher for 33 years at Newport Highschool. He has a B.A. in history from Spaulding University and a rank 1 in education from Northern Kentucky University. The students at Living Hope Baptist Church love Bill and view him as a mentor.
bill@livinghopebaptist.org
