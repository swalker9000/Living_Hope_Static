During December

Family Days

At Living Hope Baptist Church we value the time that families spend together. We believe that strong families are important for the spiritual growth of a church. Parents are the primary disciple-makers of their children, and so we occasionally take breaks from certain services and ministries in order for families to fellowship and grow together. As such, we are cancelling our Wednesday night services for the month of December to allow families to invest in each other.
